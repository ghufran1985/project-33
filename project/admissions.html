Admissions at Pakistan International School

Pakistan International School offers a smooth admission process ensuring that every student has a chance to join our academic community.

Easy Admission Process

Our school offers a straightforward admissions process. Students only need to take an entrance exam designed to assess their knowledge and skills needed to succeed at PISQ.

Moreover we emphasize student choice. Our students can select subjects that match their personal interests and if they feel a subject is not the right they have the opportunity to change it within the first month of the school.

We also conduct an interview with each student to understand their background, goals ensuring that we provide the best environment for their growth.

Explore Our Campus

Our spacious campus provides a stimulating environment for students to grow academically and socially.

End of Year Admissions Details

As the school year progresses we want to remind you that the admissions process remains simple. Entry into Pakistan International School is easy for the students who are ready to excel. However, there is an important aspect to keep in mind: the entrance exam. This exam, while designed to be straightforward, allows us to ensure students are well-prepared for the academic journey.

On exam days, we have early school hours to help students adjust and ensure a smooth start. Students should arrive early and be well-rested to perform their best.
