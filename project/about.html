About Us

At PISQ Cambridge we offer top-tier education, focusing on both academic excellence and personal growth. Our modern facilities include state-of-the-art football grounds, well-equipped science labs, and a team of passionate teachers.

Following the Cambridge curriculum, we prepare students for future success, giving them essential skills, knowledge, and the ability to thrive in a rapidly changing world.

Clubs and Extracurricular Activities

We believe that education goes beyond the classroom. Our extracurricular activities help students develop new interests, leadership skills, and a sense of community. Here are some of the clubs we offer:

  • Debate Club: Sharpen your public speaking and critical thinking skills.
  • Sports Club: Play football, basketball, cricket, badminton, and volleyball.
  • Science Club: Explore exciting experiments in chemistry, biology, and physics.
  • Art Club: Express your creativity through painting, sculpture, and design.
  • Drama Club: Develop performance skills through acting, band, and choir.
  • Volunteer Club: Participate in community and environmental projects.
  • Gaming Club: Build and code robots, and solve real-world challenges.
  • Computer Club: Explore coding, robotics, and technology innovations.

These clubs provide opportunities for students to grow, build lasting friendships, and gain skills that will benefit them in the future.
