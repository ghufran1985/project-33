Welcome to Pakistan International School

Pakistan International School offers a world-class education following the Cambridge curriculum helping young minds develop and preparing them for future success.

Why Choose PISQ?

At PISQ, we take pride in having some of the best students in the region. Our learners achieve outstanding results in their exams.

As one of the first Pakistani schools to establish itself in Qatar PISQ has earned a reputation for excellence over the past 20+ years. Our school offers education that meets international standards and prepares students to thrive.

Our Cambridge curriculum empowers students not only to excel academically but also to be well-prepared for the challenges of life. Whether in Science, Arts, or Sports, our students are at the forefront of education in the region.
